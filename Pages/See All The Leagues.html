THE WORLD’S GAME

Experience unrivalled authenticity in EA SPORTS™ FIFA 23 with football’s biggest stars and teams. Take to the pitch as 19,000+ players across 700+ teams in more than 100 stadiums and in over 30 leagues, including women’s club teams for the first time in EA SPORTS FIFA history.

FIFA 23 is the only place you can play on the world’s biggest stage in both the men’s and women’s FIFA World Cup™ tournaments, as well as the iconic UEFA Champions League, UEFA Europa League, UEFA Europa Conference League, CONMEBOL Libertadores, CONMEBOL Sudamericana, Premier League, Bundesliga, and LaLiga Santander.

ONLY IN FIFA 23

Lift the iconic FIFA World Cup™ or UEFA Champions League trophy, take your team to UEFA Europa League or UEFA Europa Conference League glory, experience the intensity of South American football in the CONMEBOL Libertadores, beat the competition to reach the top of the Premier League, and revel in the atmosphere of the Bundesliga and the energy of LaLiga Santander.

JUVENTUS RETURNS

They’re back. Play as the stars of Italy’s most storied club with authentic 2022/23 kits in the bouncing Allianz Stadium atmosphere as Juventus return to the game in FIFA 23.


EA SPORTS FIFA 23 is the only place where you can experience the competition, energy and excitement of the world's most popular league. Play with all 20 Premier League clubs, complete with their 2022/23 kits and logos and an authentic broadcast, commentary, and pre-match choreography package making it the most authentic representation of the league; exclusive to FIFA 23 players.


From Career Mode to Kick-Off, Atlético Madrid to Real Madrid, play with the biggest stars from all 20 LaLiga Santander clubs throughout every mode, exclusively in FIFA 23. The impressive broadcast package complete with 360-degree replays will further immerse you in every aspect of LaLiga atmosphere.


Nowhere else can you play as all 18 Bundesliga clubs with authentic squads, kits, and crests. In FIFA 23, enjoy full and exclusive Bundesliga authenticity with accurate recreations of the league’s biggest names like Alphonso Davies and Jude Bellingham. An authentic broadcast package, complete with exclusive branding and immersive stadium atmospheres brings you even closer to the action, whichever team you play with.


From Milan to Turin, Rome to Naples, play as all of Serie A TIM’s biggest stars, and enjoy all the atmosphere, energy, passion, and prestige of Italy’s historic top flight. An exclusive partnership between EA SPORTS and Lega Serie A brings more of the unique characteristics of Italian football to the game, with an EA SPORTS Player of the Month award celebrating the league’s top performers each month.


From the passion of the Parc des Princes to the glory of Lyon’s Groupama Stadium, FIFA 23 brings you the action, authenticity and drama of France’s prestigious Ligue 1 Uber Eats. With all the Ligue 1 teams, players, and fully authentic matchday branding, broadcast packages and pre-match atmosphere, FIFA 23 gives you the most realistic Ligue 1 experience.


Exclusively in FIFA 23, get the most authentic Major League Soccer experience. A renewal of the long-term partnership between EA SPORTS and MLS gives you the chance to play as all 28 teams, with the league’s biggest players, plus authentic matchday branding, broadcast package, and more.


Play The World’s Game in one of global football’s most storied locations. FIFA 23 brings you the authentic kits, squads, and clubs of the Scottish Professional Football League, including the chance to play one of the game’s most iconic matches between Celtic and Rangers.
